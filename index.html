About PaleoSpin

The Paleo Diet has become extremely popular, likely due to its simplicity and high level of success. I've seen many friends and colleagues adopt it and make lifelong changes in their health and lifestyle.

A common challenge to overcome is preventing boredom, while keeping meals fast and simple to prepare. I have long used Robb Wolf's awesome Paleo Food Matrix as my most trusted tool for this purpose. Simply pick any combination of ingredients from the matrix and you're all set to prepare thousands of different meals.

PaleoSpin is a simple, slightly modified, interactive version of Robb's matrix that will randomly select a meal for you. I hope that it will serve as my contribution back to what Paleo has given me and help someone put a quick, easy Paleo meal on their table for dinner.

PaleoSpin is open source software. If you know a thing or two about writing software and/or Paleo nutrition, please check out the GitHub page and contribute. I have lots of ideas for PaleoSpin that you could help bring to life.
